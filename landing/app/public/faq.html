Frequently asked questions

Is the app really free?

Yes. There’s a single free tier—no paid upgrades or ads.

What can I store per transaction?

Exactly four fields: Amount, Date, Company, and Description.

Do I need an account?

You sign in with Google to authenticate yourself. No separate password is required.

Can I export my data?

Check the GitHub repo for currently available features and roadmap items.

How do I report bugs or request features?

Open an issue on GitHub.
