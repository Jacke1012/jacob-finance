How it works

Jacob Finance is built for speed and clarity. There’s just one job: capture the essentials of each transaction—nothing more, nothing less.

1. Sign in with Google

Click Sign in with Google inside the app and approve access. We only use Google to authenticate you—no posting, no email spam.

2. Add a transaction

Hit New and fill four fields: Amount, Date, Company, Description. Save. That’s it.

3. See your list instantly

New entries appear in your table immediately so you can skim, sort, or find what you just added.

4. Free forever

FREE TIER The app is completely free. There are no paid tiers or hidden upgrades.

What you can record

  • Amount — e.g., 199.00
  • Date — the transaction date
  • Company — merchant or payer
  • Description — any details you want to remember

No categories, budgets, or reports to fuss with—just the core facts so you can stay consistent.

Privacy & security—in plain language

We keep things simple and respectful. Authentication is via Google. We don’t use your data for marketing. Always use a secure device and keep your Google account safe.

  • Authentication: Google sign‑in verifies you. No social posting, ever.
  • Your entries: You record amount, date, company, description for your own tracking.
  • Transport security: The site is served over HTTPS.
  • Transparency: The codebase is public. Review it, file issues, contribute on GitHub.

Questions about how your data is handled? Check the repository and open an issue.

FAQ

Is the app really free?

Yes. There’s a single free tier—no paid upgrades.

What can I store per transaction?

Exactly four fields: Amount, Date, Company, and Description.

Do I need an account?

You sign in with Google to authenticate yourself. No separate password is required.

Can I see or edit my entries later?

After saving, your transactions appear in a table for quick review. Editing is done with a simple click of the edit button

Where can I report bugs or request features?

Open an issue on GitHub.

Ready to log your first transaction?

Open the app, sign in with Google, and add your first entry—it takes about 10 seconds.
