How it works

Jacob Finance is built for speed and clarity. There’s just one job: capture the essentials of each transaction—nothing more, nothing less.

1. Sign in with Google

Click Sign in with Google in the app and approve access. We only use Google to authenticate you—no posting, no email spam.

2. Add a transaction

Hit New and fill four fields: Amount, Date, Company, Description. Save. That’s it.

3. See your list instantly

New entries appear in your table immediately so you can skim, sort, or find what you just added.

4. Free forever

FREE TIER The app is completely free. There are no paid tiers or hidden upgrades.
