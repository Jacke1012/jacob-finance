Privacy & security—in plain language

Authentication

We use Google sign‑in to verify you. No social posting, no email spam.

Transport

The site is served over HTTPS to protect traffic in transit.

Your entries

You record amount, date, company, and description for your own tracking. See the public code for details.

Transparency

The codebase is public. Review it, file issues, contribute on GitHub.

Questions about how your data is handled? Check the repository and open an issue: github.com/Jacke1012/jacob-finance.
